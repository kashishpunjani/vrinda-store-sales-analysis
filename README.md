📊 Vrinda Store Sales Analysis (Excel Dashboard)
📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing e-commerce sales data of Vrinda Store (2022) to identify trends, customer behavior, and business performance using Excel.

The goal was to convert raw data into meaningful insights that can support better decision-making.

❗ Problem Statement

The business had raw sales data but:

No clear visibility of monthly performance trends
No understanding of customer segments (age, gender)
No identification of top-performing states and channels
No tracking of order issues (returns, cancellations)

👉 Result: Decisions were not data-driven

🛠️ Tools & Techniques Used
Microsoft Excel
Pivot Tables
Data Cleaning
Data Visualization (Charts & Dashboard)
📂 Dataset Details

The dataset includes:

Customer Info: Gender, Age, Age Group
Order Info: Order ID, Status, Date
Product Info: Category, SKU, Size
Sales Info: Amount, Quantity
Location: City, State
Channel: Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, etc.
📈 Key Analysis Performed
1. Sales vs Orders (Monthly Trend)
Identified peak sales in March (~1.92M)
Continuous decline till November (~1.61M)

👉 Indicates possible seasonal demand or retention issue

2. Customer Segmentation
Women contribute ~64% of total revenue
Men contribute ~36%

👉 Business is highly dependent on women customers

3. Age Group Analysis
Adult women are the highest buyers (34.59%)
Teenagers also contribute significantly

👉 Target audience is clearly defined

4. Top Performing States
Maharashtra (₹2.99M)
Karnataka (₹2.64M)

👉 Sales concentrated in limited regions

5. Order Status Analysis
Delivered: 92%
Returned + Cancelled + Refunded: ~8%

👉 Operational performance is good but still has improvement scope

6. Sales by Channel
Amazon: 35.5% (highest)
Myntra & Flipkart also major contributors

👉 Heavy dependency on marketplaces

📊 Key Insights
Sales drop observed after March → needs investigation
Majority revenue from women → limited diversification
High reliance on Amazon → business risk
Sales concentrated in few states → expansion opportunity
📌 Conclusion

This dashboard helps in:

Understanding customer behavior
Tracking sales performance
Identifying risks and opportunities

👉 Provides a strong base for business decision-making
